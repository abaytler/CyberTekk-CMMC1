What is CMMC?

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) is to normalize and standardize cybersecurity practices and prepare contractors/subcontractors and businesses against malicious attackers, assess their capabilities, readiness and sophistication in the area of cybersecurity. It's created by the United States Department of Dense (DoD) and consists of 5 levels. According to the DoD, CMMC will be required for all government contracted companies by early 2026 to be fully compliant. This certification is also required in order to compete against new bids.
